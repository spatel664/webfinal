About the creator


I am Sruhsti Patel, I've chosen to build a website dedicated to the classic game, Rock, Paper, Scissors. This game, despite its simplicity, has a global fanbase, offering the opportunity to connect with a diverse audience. My website will delve into the game's strategies, explore its intriguing history across cultures, and even highlight its utility as a decision-making tool. Furthermore, it seeks to foster a community where enthusiasts can share their unique takes on the the game, engage in discussions, and participate in online tournaments. By incorporating game updates and potential themed merchandise, the website is designed to be an engaging, fun-filled resource, blending my love for Rock, Paper, Scissors with my passion for web development.
